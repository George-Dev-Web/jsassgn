About Akan Culture and Names
In Akan culture, names are deeply significant and are given based on the day of the week a person is born, along with their gender. Each day is associated with a unique Akan name, which carries its own meaning and history. By using this form, you can discover your Akan name based on your birthdate and gender, and connect with an important part of Ghanaian tradition.
